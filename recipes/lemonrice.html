Odin's Lemon Rice Recipe

Lemon Rice, my favourite

Sometimes after cooking for Thor and Loki, I end up with having too much left-over Rice and it would be a waste to throw that all away. So I decided to find a way to use that efficiently and the end result? LEMON RICE!

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Cooking Oil
  • Peanuts
  • Lemons

Steps:

  1. Wash and soak half a cup of rice in a bowl for at least 15 minutes.
  2. Create tempering by heating half a spoon of oil in a pan
  3. Roast 4 spoons of peanuts until crunchy and deep golden.
  4. Squeeze lemon all over the rice and add peanuts
